Breed

Description

Charteux


  • "Smiling Cat of France", named after Charteuse (Liqueur).

  • Weight: M - 10-15lbs, F - 6-11lbs

  • Matures in 3-5yrs.

Munchkin


  • Is a result of a mutation during WWII, hence being known for its short legs.

  • Weight: 5-9lbs

  • The breed was established in 1983.

Somali


  • Result of repopulating Abyssinians during WWII.

  • Weight: M - 10-12lbs, F - 6-10lbs

  • Named after East African region, Somalia.
